Ingredient for Fried Rice

  • Meat of your choice
  • Vegetable of your choice
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

    • How to cook fried rice

  • Stir-fried meat and vegetable
  • After that add rice (preferably cold rice)
  • Seasoning with salt, soy sauce, and pepper as you like
